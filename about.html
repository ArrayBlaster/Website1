Guess we are what some may say- "A dream come true". But What do you do when you have got not one but a few dreams. And that's what Lost Art Studio is. Not just a dream but one of the dreams from the founders dream.

We are a startup from the northeastern state of India, TRIPURA. With Dreams to connect the Colorful minds of the state with rest of the country. The state has always been in the background and I wouldn't be startled to hear that a number of Indians don't even know that there is a state with this name in then state. But No matter where we come from, You will all be stunned to see how colorful the minds of the people from the state are. Not that It's just us. Ever mind is colorful. A Most of the minds to get to see that and a way to showcase. But not all are that lucky. And hence, here we are. To let it all flow out to add new colors to our motherland India. And in doing so we will also be helping in braking a stereo typical idea that the states in the northeastern regions are poor and have nothing good to offer to the country.
